# Foundations - Learning Resources

Curated resources for learning how the internet works, networking fundamentals, data structures, and computer science basics.

## YouTube Channels & Tutorials

| Topic | Channel/Video | Level | Why Watch It |
|-------|--------------|-------|--------------|
| How the Internet Works | Fireship - "The Internet Explained" | Beginner | Fast-paced, visual explanation of internet infrastructure in under 15 minutes |
| Networking Fundamentals | NetworkChuck - Networking Series | Beginner | Energetic, hands-on approach to TCP/IP, DNS, and HTTP |
| Data Structures | CS Dojo - Data Structures Series | Beginner | Clear, whiteboard-style explanations with Python examples |
| Algorithms | Abdul Bari - Algorithms Playlist | Intermediate | Thorough walkthroughs of sorting, searching, and graph algorithms |
| Computer Science Crash Course | CrashCourse - Computer Science | Beginner | 40-episode series covering CS from gates to AI |
| How DNS Works | PowerCert Animated Videos | Beginner | Simple animations explaining DNS resolution step by step |
| Operating Systems | Jenny's Lectures - OS Playlist | Intermediate | University-quality lectures on processes, memory, and scheduling |
| Binary & Data Representation | Ben Eater - Building a Computer | Advanced | Hardware-level understanding of how data flows in a CPU |
| HTTP Deep Dive | Hussein Nasser - HTTP/1 vs HTTP/2 vs HTTP/3 | Intermediate | Practical comparison of protocol versions with real examples |
| Web Security Basics | Computerphile - Security Playlist | Intermediate | Accessible explanations from university researchers |

## Recommended Books

| Topic | Book | Author | Level | Why Read It |
|-------|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Computer Science | "Code: The Hidden Language of Computer Hardware and Software" | Charles Petzold | Beginner | Builds understanding from flashlights to CPUs — no prerequisites needed |
| Algorithms | "Grokking Algorithms" | Aditya Bhargava | Beginner | Visual, illustrated guide to algorithms for non-CS backgrounds |
| Networking | "Computer Networking: A Top-Down Approach" | Kurose & Ross | Intermediate | The standard networking textbook, starts from applications down to physical layer |
| Operating Systems | "Operating Systems: Three Easy Pieces" | Remzi & Andrea Arpaci-Dusseau | Intermediate | Free online textbook with clear writing and practical examples |
| Data Structures | "Introduction to Algorithms" (CLRS) | Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein | Advanced | The definitive algorithms reference — dense but comprehensive |
| Internet Architecture | "TCP/IP Illustrated, Volume 1" | W. Richard Stevens | Advanced | Deep dive into how internet protocols actually work on the wire |
| Computer Architecture | "Computer Organization and Design" | Patterson & Hennessy | Intermediate | Understand how hardware executes your software |
| Mathematics for CS | "Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications" | Kenneth Rosen | Intermediate | The math foundation behind algorithms, logic, and proofs |
