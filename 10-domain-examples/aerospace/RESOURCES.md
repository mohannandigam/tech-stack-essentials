# Aerospace - Learning Resources

Curated resources for learning aerospace software engineering, flight systems, satellite operations, and aviation technology.

## YouTube Channels & Tutorials

| Topic | Channel/Video | Level | Why Watch It |
|-------|--------------|-------|--------------|
| Rocket Science | Everyday Astronaut - "How Engines Work" Series | Beginner | Complex rocket propulsion explained with amazing visuals |
| Orbital Mechanics | Scott Manley - Orbital Mechanics Playlist | Beginner | Former astrophysicist explains orbits using Kerbal Space Program |
| Aviation Systems | Mentour Pilot - Aviation Systems | Beginner | Real airline pilot explains cockpit systems and procedures |
| SpaceX Engineering | Primal Space - SpaceX Technology | Beginner | How Falcon 9, Starship, and Dragon systems work |
| ADS-B & Radar | VASAviation - ATC Communications | Beginner | Real air traffic control audio with radar visualizations |
| Flight Dynamics | Prof. Mark Drela (MIT OCW) - Flight Dynamics | Advanced | MIT lectures on aircraft performance and stability |
| Satellite Systems | Smarter Every Day - Space Engineering | Beginner | Hands-on exploration of satellite and launch systems |
| DO-178C | AdaCore - Safety-Critical Software | Advanced | Certification and safety-critical software development practices |
| GPS Technology | Ben Eater - "How GPS Works" | Intermediate | Hardware-level explanation of GPS signal processing |
| Space Debris | European Space Agency - Clean Space | Intermediate | Space sustainability and debris mitigation strategies |

## Recommended Books

| Topic | Book | Author | Level | Why Read It |
|-------|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Space Mission Design | "Space Mission Engineering: The New SMAD" | Wertz, Everett, Puschell | Advanced | The definitive reference for space mission architecture and design |
| Orbital Mechanics | "Orbital Mechanics for Engineering Students" | Howard Curtis | Intermediate | Kepler's laws to interplanetary trajectories — clear and mathematical |
| Aviation Systems | "Avionics: Development and Implementation" | Cary Spitzer | Advanced | How avionics systems are designed, certified, and integrated |
| Flight Dynamics | "Introduction to Flight" | John Anderson | Beginner | Accessible introduction to how aircraft fly — from Wright brothers to modern jets |
| Safety-Critical Software | "Software Engineering for Safety-Critical Systems" | Neil Storey | Advanced | DO-178C compliance and safety-critical development practices |
| Satellite Communications | "Satellite Communications" | Dennis Roddy | Intermediate | Link budgets, orbits, and communication system design |
| Air Traffic Management | "Air Traffic Management: Economics, Regulation and Governance" | Daniel Arblaster | Intermediate | How the ATC system works as a business and technology platform |
| Space Debris | "Space Debris: Models and Risk Analysis" | Heiner Klinkrad | Advanced | Mathematical models for debris tracking and collision probability |
