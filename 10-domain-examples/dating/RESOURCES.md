# Dating Platform Engineering - Learning Resources

Curated resources for learning about recommendation systems, geospatial engineering, real-time messaging, and marketplace dynamics as they apply to dating platforms.

## YouTube Channels & Tutorials

| Topic | Channel/Video | Level | Why Watch It |
|-------|--------------|-------|--------------|
| Recommendation Systems | StatQuest - Collaborative Filtering | Beginner | Clear explanation of the math behind "users like you also liked" |
| System Design: Tinder | ByteByteGo - "Design Tinder" | Intermediate | Visual architecture walkthrough of a dating platform |
| Geospatial Databases | Hussein Nasser - PostGIS Tutorial | Intermediate | Hands-on geospatial queries for location-based apps |
| WebSocket / Real-time | Fireship - "WebSockets in 100 Seconds" | Beginner | Quick intro to real-time communication for chat features |
| Matching Algorithms | Reducible - Stable Matching / Gale-Shapley | Beginner | Visual explanation of the algorithm behind Hinge's "Most Compatible" |
| Two-Sided Marketplaces | a16z - Marketplace Dynamics | Intermediate | Venture capital perspective on marketplace chicken-and-egg problems |
| Trust & Safety | Stanford HAI - Content Moderation at Scale | Advanced | Academic perspective on moderation challenges platforms face |
| Mobile Development | Fireship - React Native Tutorial | Beginner | Cross-platform mobile development used by most dating apps |

## Recommended Books

| Topic | Book | Author | Level | Why Read It |
|-------|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Marketplace Design | "Matchmakers: The New Economics of Multisided Platforms" | David Evans & Richard Schmalensee | Intermediate | The economics of two-sided marketplaces — directly applicable to dating |
| Recommendation Systems | "Practical Recommender Systems" | Kim Falk | Intermediate | Build recommendation engines from scratch with real examples |
| Choice Psychology | "The Paradox of Choice" | Barry Schwartz | Beginner | Why more options lead to less satisfaction — explains swipe fatigue |
| Behavioral Design | "Hooked: How to Build Habit-Forming Products" | Nir Eyal | Beginner | The psychology behind engagement loops and notification design |
| Geospatial Data | "PostGIS in Action" | Regina Obe & Leo Hsu | Intermediate | The definitive guide to geospatial queries with PostgreSQL |
| Trust & Safety | "Content Moderation: Managing What People Post Online" | T&S industry collective | Intermediate | Frameworks for building trust and safety systems at scale |
| Platform Strategy | "Platform Revolution" | Geoffrey Parker et al. | Intermediate | How platforms create value — network effects, governance, and scaling |
| Real-time Systems | "Designing Real-Time Systems" | Hassan Gomaa | Advanced | Architecture patterns for low-latency messaging systems |
