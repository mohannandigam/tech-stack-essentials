## Recommended Books

| Topic | Book | Author | Level | Why Read It |
|-------|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| DevOps Culture | "The Phoenix Project" | Gene Kim, Kevin Behr, George Spafford | Beginner | A novel that makes DevOps principles click through storytelling |
| DevOps Practice | "The DevOps Handbook" | Gene Kim et al. | Intermediate | Practical companion to The Phoenix Project with implementation guides |
| Containers | "Docker Deep Dive" | Nigel Poulton | Beginner | The best introduction to Docker — clear, practical, and complete |
| Kubernetes | "Kubernetes in Action" | Marko Luksa | Intermediate | Comprehensive K8s guide from pods to operators |

| Monitoring | "Observability Engineering" | Charity Majors, Liz Fong-Jones, George Miranda | Intermediate | Modern observability practices beyond traditional monitoring |
| CI/CD | "Continuous Delivery" | Jez Humble & David Farley | Intermediate | The definitive book on build, test, and deploy automation |
| Linux | "The Linux Command Line" | William Shotts | Beginner | Comprehensive introduction to the terminal for beginners |
